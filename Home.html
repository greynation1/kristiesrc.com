Introduction

Hello, my name is Dexter. I am a miniature, long-haired, red-dapple dachshund "weiner dog". I was born on January 26, 2008 and I met my family in early March of 2008. I live with my owners David and Kristie Mason in Birmingham Alabama, but soon I will be moving to Auburn with my parents to live with my best friend Tuna. I like to run and eat and love anyone who will let me. I have never bitten anyone and am on my way to being a well behaved puppy.

I began my life a purebred miniature dachshund at a kennel in Louisville Mississippi. When I was old enough to leave my mother I was brought to a pet shop in Hoover Alabama; Wags to Riches. That is where I was discovered by my loving owners. After some persuasion by Kristie at the Baumhaurs Wings next door David agreed that I was the dog for them. One day, after a couple of months with Kristie and David, they took me to Jan and Richards and I didn't see them for a whole week. Aparently they got married and didn't tell me they were going on their honeymoon. All was well when they got back though they brought me a genuine Mexican sombrero. Soon after that they started taking me to Petsmart puppy training classes. They are a lot better at communicating with me now. These days I spend a lot of time chilling in the kitchen while they are at work and then going crazy when they get home. I dont know why I just get these sudden urges to run around like a mad dog. They get a kick out of it. Well if you want to know more check out the rest of my pages.
